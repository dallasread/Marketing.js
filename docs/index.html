Why doesn't hand-crafted code exist anymore?

Somewhere along the way, we started building complex software. Moving parts led to more moving parts, and then to more. Closures clashing with singleton scope crashes and we're left with broken pieces in our hands. Yes — of course — we're able to do more than ever with fewer lines, but code complexity continues to grow.

Don't fret; there's hope. You can join the fight against software bloat — with lesser code and fewer dependencies. Marketing.js is joining the movement with a catalog of marketing components for your website.
